Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is EDITSMYPDF?

EDITSMYPDF is a free, browser-based PDF editor and toolkit that allows you to perform various PDF operations entirely on your device. We offer tools for merging, splitting, compressing, converting, editing, and protecting PDF files - all without requiring any software installation or account creation.

Is EDITSMYPDF really free?

Yes, EDITSMYPDF is completely free. All tools are available at no cost with no hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium versions. We believe PDF editing should be accessible to everyone without financial barriers.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is needed. Simply visit EDITSMYPDF, select your desired tool, upload your PDF, and start editing. No registration, login, or personal information required.

What platforms does EDITSMYPDF support?

EDITSMYPDF works on any device with a modern web browser, including Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS, and Android. Whether you're using a desktop computer, laptop, tablet, or smartphone, you can use EDITSMYPDF.

Privacy & Security

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely. Your PDF files are processed entirely in your browser on your device. They are never uploaded to our servers, stored in any cloud service, or shared with third parties. We never access, track, or log any of your files. Your privacy is our highest priority.

Do you collect personal information?

EDITSMYPDF does not collect personal information. We don't ask for or store your name, email, location, or any identifying information. We may collect anonymous usage statistics through Google Analytics to improve our service, but this is completely anonymous.

Where is my data processed?

All PDF processing happens on your device in your browser. No data is sent to external servers. This ensures maximum privacy and security while also providing instant processing speeds.

How do you make money if the service is free?

EDITSMYPDF is supported by non-intrusive advertising through Google AdSense. We display relevant ads on our website to fund development and server costs. We don't sell user data or track personal information.

Features & Functionality

What PDF tools are available?

EDITSMYPDF offers the following tools: Merge PDFs, Split PDFs, Compress PDFs, Edit PDFs, Convert Images to PDF, Convert Text to PDF, Protect PDFs with passwords, Unlock PDFs, Rotate PDF pages, Add Watermarks, Extract Images from PDFs, and OCR (recognize text in scanned pages).

How large can my PDF files be?

The maximum file size depends on your device's available memory and browser capabilities. Most modern devices can handle PDFs ranging from 100MB to 500MB. Processing speed depends on your device's performance.

Can I process multiple PDFs at once?

You can process PDFs one at a time through our interface. For batch processing of multiple files, you would need to process each PDF individually.

What file formats do you support?

EDITSMYPDF primarily works with PDF files. Our Image to PDF tool supports common image formats (JPG, PNG, GIF, etc.), and our Text to PDF tool accepts plain text input.

Do you support password-protected PDFs?

Yes, our PDF Unlock tool can remove password protection from encrypted PDFs. Simply upload your protected PDF, and we'll help you unlock it securely on your device.

Technical Questions

Do I need to install any software?

No installation required. EDITSMYPDF is a web-based application that runs in your browser. You don't need to download, install, or configure any software.

What browsers are supported?

EDITSMYPDF works with all modern web browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. We recommend using the latest version of your browser for optimal performance.

Does EDITSMYPDF work offline?

Once loaded, EDITSMYPDF can work offline. You can load the webpage while connected to the internet, then use the tools without an active connection. However, you'll need internet to initially load the page.

Why does my PDF processing take so long?

PDF processing speed depends on your device's processor power and available memory. Larger files take longer to process. Your internet connection doesn't affect processing speed since everything happens on your device.

What if I encounter an error?

If you experience an error, please try the following: clear your browser cache, refresh the page, close other browser tabs to free up memory, or try a different browser. If the problem persists, contact us at editsmypdf@gmail.com with details about the error.

Specific Tool Questions

How do I merge multiple PDFs?

Open the Merge PDF tool, upload all PDFs you want to combine, arrange them in your desired order by dragging, and click "Merge." The combined PDF will be ready for download.

How do I compress a PDF?

Use the Compress PDF tool, upload your file, specify your target file size (optional), and the tool will reduce the PDF size while maintaining readable quality.

Can I add my signature to a PDF?

Yes, the Edit PDF tool allows you to draw signatures or shapes on your PDF. You can use your mouse, trackpad, or touch screen to add custom signatures and annotations.

How do I protect a PDF with a password?

Use the Protect PDF tool, upload your file, enter your desired password, and download the protected PDF. The password will be required to open the file.

What is the OCR feature?

OCR (Optical Character Recognition) recognizes text in scanned PDF images. This is useful for converting scanned documents into searchable text PDFs. This feature is currently in beta.
