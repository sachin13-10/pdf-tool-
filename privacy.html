Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 11, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to EDITSMYPDF ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information on our website, particularly regarding our PDF tools and the use of advertising services.

Our core principle is privacy by design. Our PDF editing tools are designed to work entirely within your browser. Your files are never uploaded, stored, or transmitted to our servers.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personal data or content from the files you use with our tools. All processing of your PDF files is done on your own computer (client-side). The content of your files remains private to you.

3. Information We Collect

While we do not collect personal data from your files, we do use third-party services that may collect data to operate our site and show ads.

  • Log Data: Like most websites, our servers may automatically log standard data provided by your web browser, such as your IP address, browser type, the pages you visit, and the time and date of your visit. This data is used for statistical purposes and to maintain site security.
  • Cookies and Web Beacons: We use cookies to help personalize your experience and for advertising purposes. A cookie is a small piece of data that a website stores on your device, which your browser provides each time you visit.

4. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements on our website.

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google collects and uses data, please see Google's Privacy & Terms.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You can refuse cookies from our site by adjusting your browser settings. Please note that refusing cookies may affect your ability to use some of our services.

6. Data Processing & GDPR

EDITSMYPDF complies with GDPR and other international data protection regulations. Since we don't collect personal data (except optional analytics), there is no personal data to process, store, or transfer. All PDF processing happens in your browser on your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
