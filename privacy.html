Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 5, 2025

Overview

This website provides client‑side PDF tools. Your files are processed locally in your browser. We do not upload your PDFs to a server or permanently store them.

What we collect

  • Operational/usage data needed to render this site and its tools.
  • Advertising cookies and similar technologies may be set by our partners (e.g., Google AdSense) to deliver and measure ads.

Files you process

PDFs and images you open in our tools remain in your browser’s memory. They are not transmitted to our servers. When you close the page, the in‑memory data is discarded.

Advertising

We may display third‑party advertisements (e.g., via Google AdSense). These partners may use cookies or similar identifiers. You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites and apps that use its services at Google’s policy page.

Analytics

If we enable privacy‑friendly analytics, we will do so in a way that avoids collecting personally identifiable information where possible.

Data retention

Because PDFs are processed client‑side, we do not retain your documents. Any logs (if enabled) exclude document contents.

Your choices

  • You can block or clear cookies in your browser settings.
  • You can use the tools without uploading files to any server.

Contact

Questions? Contact us via the details on the About page.
