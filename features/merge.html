FAQs

No. Each page keeps its original size and orientation. Mixed sizes are allowed inside one merged PDF.

Yes. We copy pages as pages, preserving embedded text, links, and vector graphics.

Yes, but it depends on your device memory. If it feels slow, merge in batches and then combine the batches.
