About EDITSMYPDF

Our Mission

At EDITSMYPDF, our mission is to provide simple, free, and accessible PDF tools that respect your privacy. We believe that powerful document editing capabilities should not require expensive software or compromise your data security.

How It Works

Our website offers a suite of tools to handle common PDF tasks, such as merging, compressing, splitting, and converting documents. What makes us different is our commitment to privacy:

  • Browser-Based: All tools run directly in your web browser.
  • No Uploads: Your files are never sent to or stored on our servers. Everything stays on your computer.
  • Completely Free: There are no hidden fees or subscriptions.

This site was created for anyone who needs a quick and secure way to manage PDF files without the risk of their documents being stored on third-party servers.

Contact Us

We are always looking to improve. If you have any feedback, questions, or feature requests, please feel free to reach out to us at editsmypdf@gmail.com.

Technology & Security

EDITSMYPDF uses modern web technologies and libraries like PDF.js and PDFKit to provide reliable PDF processing. All operations use open-source, well-tested libraries. Your data security is ensured through client-side processing - your files never leave your device.

Our Commitment

  • Privacy First: We don't collect, store, or track any user data or documents
  • Free Forever: All tools remain free with no hidden costs or premium versions
  • Open & Transparent: We believe in transparent operations and honest communication
  • Continuous Improvement: We regularly update and add new features based on user feedback
  • Reliability: Our tools are built on proven, open-source technologies
  • Accessibility: We make PDF editing accessible to everyone, regardless of technical skill

For Whom Is EDITSMYPDF?

EDITSMYPDF is designed for anyone who needs to work with PDF files. Whether you're a student needing to merge assignment files, a professional managing business documents, a small business owner handling contracts, or anyone else who works with PDFs - our free tools are here to help.
